Spris has been a staple full service pizzeria on Lincoln Road since 1998.

We’re proud to bring you a more casual version of Spris, with a new look but the same quality pizza you’ve come to love!

Spris Artisan Pizza is a fast casual concept that serves a varied selection of thin crust style pizzas, salads, paninis and desserts, in the same way they would be served in a pizzeria in Italy.

We use only high quality, natural ingredients, directly imported from Italy, or locally purchased when available.
